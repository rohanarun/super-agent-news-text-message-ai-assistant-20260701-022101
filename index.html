How a text message AI assistant works in practice

1. Send a text

Message Super like you would a human assistant: “Check my calendar and reschedule Friday’s meeting.”

2. Confirm intent

Super replies with clarifying questions or a preview before taking action. You stay in control.

3. Real computer use

The agent opens the browser or desktop, logs in, navigates, and completes the task step by step.

4. Reuse the computer-use cache

Super stores what it learned. The next time you text a similar request, the computer-use cache makes it faster and cheaper.
